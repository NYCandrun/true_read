The problem

Buyers moved to AI. The old playbook didn't follow.

People used to Google a question, click a few links, and land on your page. Now they ask an AI and read the answer. They don't click. That single habit breaks the three things B2B has always run on.

Cold outreach · Nurture

Every email sounds the same

Everyone writes outreach with AI now, so it all reads as generic and buyers ignore all of it. Yours included.

Paid search · SEM

Fewer people reach the ad

The question gets answered in a chat before the buyer ever sees your ad. You pay more for less.

Search · SEO

Ranking first stopped meaning visits

The AI answers the question right on the page, so most searches now end with no click at all.

The pattern under all three: when anyone can generate the same content and the same outreach for free, none of it persuades. Free to make turned into free to ignore.

Our view

AI can remix anything. It can't invent data that doesn't exist.

A model can rewrite every blog post ever written. It can't make up the results of a survey nobody ran. Original research, real numbers from your real buyers, is the only content that is genuinely new. So it's the one thing that still works in all three broken channels at once.
